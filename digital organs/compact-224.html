Compact 224

A complete organ, in a compact size. Versatile and refined digital organ with 4 unique sound worlds and extensive options.

Audio: Internal or External

The Compact 224 offers flexibility with internal or external high-quality audio options. The internal audio setup includes multi-channel speakers built into the compact oak furniture, featuring tweeters that enrich the sound experience.

Alternatively, the external audio option allows connection to the Content Home 8 audio system, which includes two speaker housings made of oak for a broad and full organ sound, ideal for larger rooms or chapels.

Extensive Capabilities

The organ is configurable with various options, including a standard internal setup with 2 x 30 watt amplifiers. Optional upgrades include 4 x 180 watt amplifiers and the high-end Content audio system for optimal sound placement in different environments.

You may also connect your speakers or use headphones. Additional expansions include pedalboards, swell pedals, benches with stands (metal or wooden), providing customizable flexibility for musicians.
