What is a digital organ?

Content Organs has been active for decades in building digital organs. Thanks to software developed in-house and recordings of samples, it is possible to play an extensive sample library from four different stylistic worlds. Additionally, there are options for adjusting the virtual listening positions in the (church) space. These features are included as standard with Content's home organs. With a wide range, from organs with a single manual to large four- or five-manual instruments, there is a suitable model available for every organist. Content Organs: for every organist the right organ!
