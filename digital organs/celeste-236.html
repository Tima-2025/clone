Celeste 236

The Content Celeste 236 is the most popular two-manual home organ from Content Organs! Thanks to the in-house developed Invention Technology®, it provides samples from no less than four unique sound worlds. Switch effortlessly between a German Baroque organ, a French Cavaillé-Coll organ, or one of the beautiful Dutch organs with just a button press. The touch of the standard built-in plastic keyboards with metal cores completes the experience.

Built-in 3D Surround Sound System

The Celeste 236 comes standard with an impressive built-in 3D surround system featuring 14 speakers placed across various positions in the solid oak cabinet. This advanced setup creates a lifelike spatial audio experience without the need for external speakers.

The Virtual Player Position feature lets you experience your playing from different perspectives within a church setting—behind the console, in front of the organ façade, or further in the nave—achieved simply by pressing a button, thanks to the Content Convolution Reverb with over 10 unique reverbs.

Four Unique Organ Styles

The organ features four distinct traditional organ sound worlds, allowing authentic performance of organ literature across centuries:

  • North German Baroque: Ideal for music by Bach, featuring bright, articulate sounds.
  • French Romantic: Suitable for composers such as Widor, Guilmant, and Franck.
  • Dutch organ style: Crafted for the full Dutch chorale repertoire.
  • Additional unique sound world to expand tonal possibilities.

Content Convolution Reverb

The Content Convolution Reverb is a sampled reverb offering detailed acoustic environments. Select from multiple reverb programs to customize your sound space for the most authentic and enjoyable playing and listening experience.

General Specifications

  • 2 manuals (keyboards), each with 61 notes

  • 30-note flat, radial, or radial-concave pedalboard options
  • Contemporary furniture made of solid oak
  • Built-in 3D surround sound with 14 speakers
  • Virtual Player Position (VPP) technology
  • Content Convolution Reverb module with over 10 reverb options
  • Keyboards with built-in metal core for responsive touch
  • Personal Control Center for easy adjustment and configuration
  • Optional ExtraVoice for additional orchestral and instrumental sounds
