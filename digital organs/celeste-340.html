Personal Control Center

The PCC allows you to precisely customize your playing experience including master volume, stop voicing per individual key, reverb programs, tremulant depth and speed, historical temperaments, manual compass adjustment, bellows stability, stop group memories, extravoice control (optional), and more.

Audio System & Surround Sound

The organ is equipped with an advanced 6-channel amplified multi-speaker system with integrated 3D surround sound technology, providing a magnificent spatial dispersion and realistic organ sound. The Virtual Player Position allows you to experience different sound perspectives as if playing in different locations within a church setting.
