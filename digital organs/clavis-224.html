Clavis 224

An affordable practice or living room organ with high-quality sound. Suitable for beginner and advanced organists, offering four unique sound styles to cover organ literature throughout the centuries, with tonal colors perfect for study purposes.

Adaptable to Your Own Preference

The Clavis 224 is a flexible instrument with a highly balanced sound system, featuring an advanced reverb program, Virtual Player Position (VPP), and a wide 6-channel audio setup. This ensures a transparent and captivating musical experience where each stop is distinctly audible even in full ensemble.

With the Personal Control Center (PCC), the organ can be easily adjusted and customized to personal preferences. It provides years of playing and listening pleasure in home, small hall, or music studio settings.

Four Different Organ Styles in One Organ

The organ incorporates four distinct traditional specifications of stops — Baroque, Classic, Romantic, and Symphonic. Each style includes unique, high-quality samples recorded in-house from the finest pipe organs worldwide. This versatility allows you to authentically play works from the great masters in their original sound worlds.

High-Quality Samples

Thanks to the multitude of registration possibilities, unique stop samples, and the ability to customize the organ with the PCC, the Clavis 224 is an exceptionally flexible instrument.

Specifications

  • 2 manuals (keyboards) with 61 keys each
  • 4 x 24 speaking stops (one per organ style)
  • 384 programmable presets per organ style
  • Virtual Player Position (VPP) technology
  • 6 channels and 6 speakers in the audio system
  • 30-note straight or (radial) concave pedalboard
  • 2 swell pedals for tonal dynamics
  • High-quality solid oak console with multiple color options
  • Organ bench with storage included

Stop List Highlights

The Clavis 224 includes a comprehensive stop list divided into four sections that correspond with the organ styles. Below is an overview of stops for each division:

  • Pedal: 16' Subbass, 8' Oktavbass, 4' Choralbass, 16' Posaune, 8' Trompete, coupling options
  • Hauptwerk (Great): 16' Quintadena, 8' Prinzipal, 8' Rohrflöte, mixtures, trumpet, tremulant
  • Schwellwerk (Swell): 8' Holzflöte, 8' Quintadena, flutes, mixtures, Dulcian, Vox Humana, tremulant
  • Additional Pedal: 16' Bourdon, 8' Gedekt, 8' Oktav, 16' Fagot, 8' Trompet for extended tonal range
