Cantate 346

Experience the maximum capabilities of Content Invention Technology®. Inspired by historic pipe organs, this versatile instrument delivers four unique sound worlds, allowing you to play all major organ works optimally in one extraordinary organ.

General Features

  • 3 manuals (keyboards) with touch sensitivity per manual
  • 4 unique organ styles: English Symphonic, German Baroque, French Romantic, Dutch Classic
  • 4 x 46 speaking stops (184 stops total), carefully selected from famous pipe organs worldwide
  • 304 total built-in voice samples divided equally among the four styles
  • 128 ExtraVoice orchestral voices included
  • Convolution Reverb with 10 selectable acoustical environments
  • 12 independent audio channels with dynamic 180-watt amplifiers for ultimate sound detailing
  • Solid wood cabinet showcasing expert craftsmanship
  • Illuminated music rack and pedalboard for ease of use in low light
  • Personal Control Center (PCC) for customizable sound settings and adjustments
  • Three wooden expression pedals with adjustable division assignment

Stop List Highlights

The Cantate 346 offers a comprehensive array of stops across all four divisions, featuring principals, flutes, mixtures, reeds, and unique historical colors. Below is a summarized glimpse into the stop divisions:

  • Pedal: Principal 16', Subbass 16', Octave 8', Gedackt 8', Mixtures, Trompette, Cornet, Couplers, and more
  • Ruck Positiv (Great Choir): Principals, Gedackt, Octaves, Blockflöte, Mixtures, Dulcian, Tremulant
  • Werck (Great): Principal 16', Quintadena, Flutes, Mixtures, Trommet, Tremulant
  • Ober Positiv (Swell): Principals, Rohrflöte, Nasat, Dulcian, Vox Humana, Tremulant

For the full stop list and detailed division layout, please refer to the official Content Organs Cantate 346 page.

Audio & Technology

The organ features a sophisticated 12-channel audio system, optimizing sound spatiality with surround speakers radiating to sides and top of the cabinet. The sampled convolution reverb replicates ten unique acoustical environments to match various performance spaces.

Content’s proprietary Invention Technology enables easy switching between the four organ tonal worlds, providing unmatched versatility without compromise.
