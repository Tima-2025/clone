Mondri 236

The Mondri series from Content has been a hallmark for many years. The Mondri 236 combines refinement and quality in a beautifully designed oak cabinet. Equipped with wooden drawknobs with LED indication, this home organ utilizes in-house developed Invention TechnologyÂ®, offering four complete tonal specifications to suit different musical styles. Its tall cabinet provides a stunning spatial experience with built-in 6 audio channels including surround sound.

Positive Tones & Sound Character

The Mondri 236 is a two-manual positive organ offering 36 stops distributed across Great, Swell, and Pedal divisions. Its unique 6-channel audio setup delivers every piece of music as a spatial celebration, ideal for playing or listening in your living room. The organ is available in various finishes and optional features, allowing you to experience the beauty and inspiration of a compact positive organ.

Customization & Control

The organ comes with a versatile Personal Control Center (PCC) that allows easy adjustment and customization to individual preferences. Features include LED lighting near the pedal and music stand, two wooden swell pedals, and a wooden bench with sheet music storage. The cabinet's modern design can be customized with classical moldings, a French-style organ bench, and other cabinet style variations.

A Broad Palette of Tonal Colors

Content Mondri organs provide principal voices (prestants, flutes), foundation stops (mixtures, sharps), reeds (trumpet, vox humana, trombone), and solo registers like Cornet, Vox Humana, and Sesquialter. This extensive palette offers the rich tonal colors of the ancient pipe organ.

Specifications

  • 2 manuals (keyboards), 5 octaves (C to c4) with velocity sensitivity and metal core
  • 4 unique tonal specifications of stops with 36 stops each
  • 30-note pedalboard options: straight, concave, or radial-concave
  • Console made extensively of solid oak wood
  • Built-in 3D surround sound with 6 audio channels and 12 speakers
  • Personal Control Center (PCC) for fine-tuning stops, settings, and registration
  • Convolution reverb with 10 selectable acoustical environments
  • Programmable Crescendo and presets: 1800 total presets
  • Physiologic expression pedals with assignment options
  • Optional wooden drawstops, split console, and locking roll-top
  • Dimensions (cm): Height 176.5, Width 134, Depth 93 (with pedalboard), 60 (without pedalboard)
  • Weight: approx. 98.4 kg (216 lbs)

Stops Overview

The Mondri 236 stoplist features a comprehensive collection of stops distributed across divisions that include Pedal, Hauptwerk (Great), Schwellwerk (Swell), and more, with principals, flutes, reeds, mixtures, and couplers typical of Dutch organ tradition.
