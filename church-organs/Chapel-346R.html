Chapel 346R

The Chapel 346R is one of the 3-manual models at the top of our Chapel series. Thanks to an advanced software platform, the possibilities are virtually limitless. By easily expanding the number of channels or routing and switching voices to various channels, this organ has everything for optimal calibration and performance in your (church) space. Due to the many options available for expanding the organ, this model is one of the most installed church organs by Content.

The organ as an orchestra

With a standard built-in library of over 120 solo and orchestral voices, you can bring every musical style and sonic world to life. High-quality organ sound through multi-channel audio systems

"Quality is our most important stop." This promise is one that Content Organs is proud to uphold. An included 8-channel audio system can be expanded to as many as 32 separate audio channels, allowing for the highest quality and expansive tonal representation. Since each channel has various speakers, it is possible to fulfill the desire for "each speaker having a stop." This significantly enhances the carrying capacity of the instrument, especially when it comes to accompanying congregational or choral singing.

Your organ, a harmonious unity with your church

The sound of the Cantate 346 is generated based on a so-called dry and lifelike organ sample, combined with various possibilities such as beating, pitch, volume, and reverberation. The overall tonal color that emerges can be finely adjusted to the space by the organist (in consultation with the Content voicer) during installation. This is similar to the process of analog voicing and physical adjustment that takes place during the installation or revision of a pipe organ. In this way, the digital organ forms a harmonious unity with the church space, enhancing collaboration with singers, both collectively and as a unified presence in one space.
