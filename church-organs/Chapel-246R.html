The organ as your orchestra

This model demonstrates that an organ can not only sound like an organ but also come alive as an orchestra. With a standard built-in library of over 120 solo and orchestral voices, you can bring every musical style and sonic world to life. The usage of these voices depends entirely on the liturgical context. Whether accompanying choir with piano sounds or playing alongside with solo voices in an ensemble, this Content Chapel has it all.

Various organ traditions

Between continents and countries, large cultural differences exist in liturgical use and congregational singing tradition with the organ. It is important that an organ aligns with the cultural tradition in your church, as accompanying sometimes very large congregations is critical. Dutch-styled organs emphasize carrying power and foundation, with specific voiced stops suitable for accompaniment. Thanks to years of experience with authentic sampling, Content Organs offers an exceptionally extensive digital sample library featuring many Dutch organs and specific Dutch stops such as the Cornet, making this digital organ an optimal alternative to a pipe organ for Dutch churches.
