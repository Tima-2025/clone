Our References | Content Organs

On this page, you can get acquainted with a small selection of the projects we have completed in the past period. The references are displayed in alphabetical order by country. If you would like more information about a specific project, please feel free to contact our sales department.

References - U

United States - Redeemer Lutheran Church, Tucson, Arizona

This Content Chapel 354 is equipped with more than 16 speakers and was installed in this church in Tucson, Arizona. With the speakers arranged around the historic pipe façade, the organists can make full use of the instrument’s tonal depth to support both congregation and choir.

United States - Redeemer Lutheran Church, Fridley, Minnesota

The Content Concerto 476, equipped with an extensive speaker system, now serves in this Lutheran church in Fridley, Minnesota. With over 20 speakers distributed between 'Main' and 'Antiphonal', organists have all the necessary tools to accompany the congregation and choir.
