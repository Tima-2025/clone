Chapel 236R

The Chapel 236R offers organists a high degree of flexibility and extensive audio capabilities. The audio is distributed over 6 channels, with an optional internal monitor system. Additional speakers or subwoofers can also easily be connected. Intoning to personal preferences is possible via the Personal Control Center, allowing adjustments of volumes on register or key level and modification of the character of individual registers.

A complete church organ, with a robust sound

The Content Chapel 236R is available in a model with illuminated TAB registers displayed above the keyboards. This organ comes standard with 36 stops, distributed across the Great, Swell, and Pedal divisions. Each stop contains four samples playable through four distinct tonal worlds. The voicings are compiled from highly sampled pipe organs representing various style periods and countries including the Netherlands, Germany, England, and France. The sound character is described as open, fresh, and resonant. The robust oak cabinet with roll-top gives the console a sturdy and contemporary appearance.

ExtraVoice - A multifunctional instrument

In addition to the 36 speaking stops in four organ styles, this organ offers the option to add ExtraVoice stops, including piano, trumpet, dynamic orchestra, and pan flute, all playable with dynamic and touch-sensitive keyboards. This feature adds a new dimension to your musical experience, particularly useful in innovative liturgical settings, making the Chapel 236R suitable for church or concert hall applications.

Four organ styles

Like other models in the Chapel series, the Chapel 236R offers four different organ styles: North German Baroque, Dutch Classical, French Romantic, and English Symphonic. One button press switches styles, changing the sample sound while stop labels remain constant. For example, a Prestant 8' in Dutch style becomes a Prinzipal 8' in North German Baroque or a Diapason 8' in English Symphonic styles.
