Chapel 227R

The base model in Content Organs' church series is the versatile Chapel 227R. This organ is equipped with two manuals and a 30-note pedal. The organ features 27 stops with high sound quality. The pedal, equipped with 7 stops, provides a solid foundation beneath the robust Great division. With the extensive Swell division, the organist has opportunities to create dynamics and explore various tonal colors.

An organ for schools, churches and more!

The Chapel 227R comes in two speaker system options: internally integrated speakers within the cabinet or an external audio system for larger venues. This organ is recommended for auditoriums, community spaces, small churches, and funeral chapels. The suitability depends entirely on your space, and Content Organs specialists are happy to advise.

Four organ styles

Like other models in the Chapel series, the Chapel 227R offers four distinct organ styles: North German Baroque, Dutch Classical, French Romantic, and English Symphonic. With a single button press, you can switch between styles; the sample sound changes while the stop label remains the same. For example, a Prestant 8' in Dutch style becomes Prinzipal 8' in North German Baroque and Diapason 8' in English Symphonic.

Craftsmanship and cabinet quality

From Europe to Asia and beyond, organists worldwide appreciate Content-built organs for their distinctive sound and digital perfection, rooted in craftsmanship and knowledge. The high-quality organ cabinet is crafted with carefully selected wood types and traditional stylish moldings. Reliability and solidity are of utmost importance; Content organs are developed by and for organ enthusiasts globally.
