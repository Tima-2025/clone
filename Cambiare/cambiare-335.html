Cambiare 335

The Cambiare 335 follows the well-known Mondri style of Content Organs. These cabinet models have been among the best-selling for over ten years. Thanks to a narrow and elegant design, this organ doesn’t take up more space than necessary, while the furniture itself is an absolute eye-catcher. The Cambiare 335 features an extensive internal audio system with built-in surround dispersion. You can expand this model with additional internal speakers. It is delivered without the Content User Interface by default, allowing direct access to Hauptwerk™ or other VPO samplers on one or two optional touchscreens on sliding trays. This provides all options to adjust sample set settings, fully calibrated during installation. The Cambiare 335 is available in any desired oak finish, with multiple options.

High-End Cabinet from Real Oak Wood

Content’s organ furniture is crafted from European oak – cultivated by our own carpentry to guarantee the best quality. Solid oak construction ensures the organs are available in various lacquer, oil, or stain colors, so your organ perfectly complements any interior or future décor. Skilled carpenters carefully assemble and finish every organ from compact models to grand exclusive custom builds.

Easy Operation, Authentic Experience… Touchscreens

The Cambiare 335 is easy to operate with large 17” touchscreens. One or two screens can be slid out from the cabinet and positioned at will, offering authentic organ-playing experiences. Content’s high-quality standards guarantee touchscreens incorporate the latest technology.

Play Famous Pipe Organs!

Virtual Pipe Organs

With Virtual Pipe Organ (VPO) software, you can play well-known church organs at home or in the studio. Content offers a comprehensive range of VPO organs in the Cambiare collection, currently supporting Hauptwerk™ and Sweelinq samplers. Hundreds of organs worldwide become accessible for your personal play. Experience the possibilities at your dealer or at Content’s Hauptwerk Experience Center in Ermelo.
