Cambiare MIDI Console

The Cambiare MIDI Console allows you to play Hauptwerk™ or other Virtual Pipe Organ (VPO) samplers using your own computer and audio setup. Featuring a beautiful solid oak cabinet crafted with over forty years of organ-building expertise, this console combines classic craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver a high-quality playing experience.

Custom Setup and Connections

The Cambiare MIDI Console is designed for simple and straightforward connection to your own computer and audio systems. A convenient hinged door on the side provides easy access to a built-in computer compartment. The console seamlessly integrates with the Content Controller to facilitate connection setup.

The overall audio setup is created using your preferred audio interfaces, amplifiers, and speakers. Please note that Content Organs does not provide configuration support for external equipment.

Play Famous Pipe Organs Worldwide

Content offers a comprehensive range of virtual pipe organs (VPO) through the Cambiare organ collection. Compatible with Hauptwerk™ and Sweelinq samplers, the Cambiare MIDI Console gives access to hundreds of organ samples from famous organs around the world.

Experience limitless musical possibilities in your own home by scheduling a visit with one of our dealers or at the Hauptwerk Experience Center located at the Content organs workshop in Ermelo, Netherlands.

Solid and Reliable Furniture

The Cambiare MIDI Console is built using carefully selected European oak. As part of Content's in-house carpentry, the wood is cultivated and treated to ensure the highest quality and durability. The oak cabinet is available in various finishes, including lacquer, oil, and stain, making it adaptable to any interior or future décor.

Skilled carpenters assemble and finish each console daily, ranging from compact models to grand exclusive custom designs, guaranteeing reliability and elegance.

  • Optional integrated touchscreens for control
  • Easy connection to your own computer and audio systems
