Cambiare 325

Contemporary design and easy operability come together in the Cambiare 325. Due to the width of the organ console, large 17-inch touchscreens are built on both sides of the music desk. The Cambiare 325 features an extensive internal audio system with built-in surround radiation. It is also possible to expand this model with additional internal speakers.

The Cambiare 325 is delivered as standard without the Content User Interface (as with the Cambiare 315, etc.), allowing you direct access to the Hauptwerk™ software or other VPO samplers. This provides you with all the possibilities to adjust the settings of various sample sets. Of course, the chosen sample sets are fully calibrated during installation. The Cambiare 325 is available in any desired color on oak. Additionally, keyboard upgrades and the addition of a 32-note pedal are possible.

Famous pipe organs in your living room

Virtual Pipe Organs

With Virtual Pipe Organ (VPO) software, you can play well-known church organs in your living room or music studio. Content offers a comprehensive range of VPO organs with a wide variety of models in the Cambiare organ collection. Currently, VPO samplers Hauptwerk™ and Sweelinq are being used. Through both systems, a collection of hundreds of organs from around the world is available.

Convince yourself of the possibilities that playing VPO organs can offer, and make an appointment with one of our dealers or visit our Hauptwerk Experience Center in our organ workshop in Ermelo.

High-end touchscreens for authentic experience & easy operation

Thanks to two large 17-inch built-in touchscreens, the Cambiare 325 is easy to operate. You can purchase, load, and display an almost infinite library of sample sets in an organized way.

For example, you can display the organ of the Laurenskerk in Rotterdam entirely according to the original layout of stops on the left and right of the console. This way, playing the organ becomes a truly authentic experience.

Due to Content's high-quality standards, the touchscreens are always equipped with the latest developments in the world of touchscreens.
