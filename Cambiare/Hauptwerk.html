Hauptwerk™ brings pipe organs to life!

Hauptwerk™ is a software solution that enables you to bring the sound of world-famous pipe organs into your home. These organ sounds are meticulously recorded pipe by pipe, stop by stop, and in various combinations, capturing variations in microphone placement for the most accurate and lifelike digital representation of a pipe organ.

Sample sets are collections of these recordings, allowing organists worldwide to play famous and lesser-known pipe organs in the comfort of their own homes.

What is required to play Hauptwerk™?

  1. A powerful computer is required to handle the complex audio processing and simultaneous actions when playing.
  2. A reliable, durable, and solid organ console is essential for years of playing enjoyment.
  3. Control via touchscreen or drawknobs for easy operation of the Hauptwerk software.
  4. A good audio system is invaluable for the right sound experience, including high-quality amplification and speakers.

Digital organ or a Hauptwerk organ?

The main difference is in how the sound is produced. A Hauptwerk organ uses pre-recorded samples from real churches, allowing you to experience the organ as it sounds in its original space. A digital organ combines samples from various churches to create different organ styles, with options to customize the sound to your preferences.
