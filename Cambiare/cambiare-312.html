Cambiare 312

The renewed Cambiare 312 features two high-quality 10-inch touchscreens for improved operation and an updated, sophisticated audio system. This organ now supports a 32-note radial concave pedalboard and remains perfect for living rooms, offering rich sounds from famous pipe organs via Hauptwerk and Sweelinq samplers.

Virtual Player Position

The Cambiare 312 offers you the ability to control reverb and sound positioning within different church spaces using an in-house developed 'Position' application. With a slider, you can 'walk through the church' and hear the organ from the back, middle, or close to the pipes and manuals, creating a true surround experience built into your organ.

User-Friendly Operation

The Content User Interface on the two touchscreens makes controlling Hauptwerk simple and intuitive. All functions including registration, pitch adjustment, recording, reverb settings, and listening position are easily accessible at your fingertips.

High-End Audio System

The upgraded audio system ensures playback of the most beautiful pipe organ sounds at a highly refined level, due to carefully selected speakers and innovative technology. You have the flexibility to customize your organ sound to suit your preferences, delivering an unparalleled musical experience.
