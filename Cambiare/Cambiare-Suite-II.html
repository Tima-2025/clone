Cambiare Suite II

With the Content Cambiare Suite II, playing church organs in the living room becomes accessible to every organist. The combination of advanced developments from Content's organ workshop and the diverse collection of sample sets via Sweelinq's sampler leads to a new and high-quality model in the Cambiare collection. Affordability and class go hand in hand with the Cambiare Suite II.

Durability, Elegance, and Quality

The organ furniture is entirely made in oak finish and available in an extensive collection of stain, oil, or lacquer colors to perfectly complement your interior. The Cambiare Suite offers the ideal combination of solidity, elegance, and quality.

Cabinet and Audio

This model features a high-quality multichannel audio system fully integrated into the organ furniture, removing the need for external speakers in your living room. The integrated audio system delivers a realistic and rich spatial sound experience.

Play Famous Pipe Organs!

Content Organs has its own unique vision of sound reproduction, build quality, and service for decades. When playing Virtual Pipe Organs (VPO), collaboration takes place with various providers of samplers and sample sets, such as Hauptwerk™ and Sweelinq. You can virtually play famous or lesser-known pipe organs in the comfort of your own home.

Operation by Two Touchscreens

The Cambiare Suite II and Suite III are equipped with two integrated 10” touchscreens. Thanks to their size and high touch definition, it is possible to fully display and conveniently control each sample set. With simple buttons, you can switch between different sample sets or add new ones. Sweelinq also allows recording your performance and saving/recalling selected combinations (presets). These can optionally be recalled using illuminated thumb pistons.

Specifications

  • Easy operation via two 10” high-definition touchscreens
