Cambiare Suite III

With the Content Cambiare Suite III, playing church organs in the living room becomes possible for every organist. The combination of advanced developments from Content's organ factory and the diverse collection of sample sets via the Sweelinq sampler results in a new and high-quality model in the Cambiare collection. Affordability and class go hand in hand with the Cambiare Suite III. The Cambiare Suite is also available in a version with two keyboards, the Cambiare Suite II.

Durability, Elegance, and Quality

The organ cabinet is entirely made in real oak and available in an extensive collection of stain, oil, or paint colors to complement your interior perfectly. The Cambiare Suite offers the ideal combination of solidity, elegance, and quality.

Cabinet and Audio

This model features a high-quality multi-channel audio system fully integrated into the organ cabinet, eliminating the need to connect external speakers in the living room. This innovative audio system delivers a realistic and rich spatial sound experience.

Advanced User Interface

The Cambiare Suite III is equipped with two integrated 10" touchscreens, allowing effortless operation of all controls and sample sets via the Sweelinq interface. This user-friendly system enables quick switching between multiple sample sets and convenient control of registrations and presets.

Specifications

  • 3 keyboards (manuals)
  • Standard with two 10" high-resolution touchscreens
  • Ultimate sound experience through high-end audio protocol
  • Multi-functional audio system fully integrated into the organ cabinet
  • Infinitely adjustable convolution reverb
  • User-friendly operation with Sweelinq interface
  • Easy switching between different sample sets
  • Contemporary designed furniture with solid oak cabinet
  • Available in wide variety of stain, oil, or paint finishes
