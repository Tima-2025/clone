Cambiare 323

The Cambiare 323 is characterized by its unique external sound system, designed by a team of audio specialists. The speakers are housed in elegant French oak wooden casings, allowing flexible placement to maximize the spatial experience of Hauptwerk™ virtual pipe organs. This model offers a minimalist design with maximal functionality, making it an ideal study organ for every organist.

Key Features

  • Four manuals (keyboards) for expressive playing
  • Standard one touchscreen with option for a second touchscreen; screens can be mounted horizontally or vertically
  • Ultimate sound experience through proprietary Content audio protocol with I2S technology
  • User-friendly operation with Content User Interface on touchscreens
  • Easy switching between extensive sample sets for diverse organ repertoire
  • Contemporary, minimalist design with modular and expandable multifunctional external audio system
  • Stylish and modern furniture made of solid woodwork with customizable finishes
  • Speakers housed in French oak wooden casing designed for maximum spatial sound experience with Hauptwerk™
  • Supports internet updates and installation of sound samples easily through the control panel

Audio System

The Cambiare 323's external sound system is a key highlight, featuring high-quality speakers designed by acoustics specialists. The speakers can be positioned flexibly to create an optimal spatial experience, designed to faithfully reproduce the detailed organ sounds of the Hauptwerk software. This system eliminates the need for traditional in-built speakers and provides exceptional sound clarity in any room.

User Interface & Operation

The organ is controlled via one or optionally two touchscreens housed in elegant wooden frames. The Content User Interface offers intuitive control over registration changes, sample selection, temperaments, presets, and other Einstellungen (settings). The interface's modern style displays all functions clearly and accessibly, facilitating easy operation for organists of all experience levels.

Ideal for Study and Performance

The Cambiare 323 is the perfect study organ for organists wanting to explore a wide variety of pipe organ sounds. Load famous organs such as the Müller organ from Leeuwarden or the Nancy organ, enjoy powerful principals, clear reeds, and a comprehensive range of stops from all stylistic periods. The minimalist design and flexible speaker placement allow maximum adaptation to your room and lifestyle.

Specifications Summary

  • Number of manuals: 4
  • Touchscreen control: standard one, optional second
  • Sound system: Proprietary external high-quality audio
  • User Interface: Content User Interface on touchscreens
  • Speaker housing: elegant French oak wooden casing
  • Design: contemporary, minimalist, modular audio system
  • Compatibility: Hauptwerk virtual pipe organs with easy updates
  • Additional features: Internet connectivity for updates and sample installation
